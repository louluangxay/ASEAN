About Association of Southeast Asian Nation [ASEAN]

ASEAN aims to promote political, economic and social cooperation and regional stability. ASEAN was formed in August of 1967 by Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, and Thailand. Brunei Darussalam, Vietnam, Lao PDR, Myanmar, and Cambodia joined years later bringing the total membership to 10 countries.

Population of ASEAN Countries with US and China
